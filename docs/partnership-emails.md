### Notes before sending

- Add real numbers once the board fills (e.g. "X hundred live pilot jobs,
  450+ airline factfiles") — stronger pitch, but only claim what's live.
- If any of them reply with a revenue-share or paid-feed ask, that's normal —
  decide your ceiling beforehand.
- A silent week is normal; one polite follow-up after 7–10 days, then move on.
